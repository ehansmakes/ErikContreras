Project 3 - Micro-Planter

This is a current project that I am working on for my Design MFA thesis. More updates to come!

The core motivation for this project was to tackle the issue of recycling cooking appliances such as microwaves, ovens, stoves. By the time these objects reach the dumpsite, the appeal to repair and reuse these items is diminished, due to their bulky nature and the health concerns with cooking items in appliances that have spent time at a dump or recycling plant.

Project 2 - Arduino Teletyper

This is a Smith Corona Typewriter that has been modified to automatically type out: messages, and ASCII art pieces. The components are housed in a Cisco 2600 Series router enclosure.

The goal of this project was to maintain the original 1994 functionality and aesthetic of the typewriter while incorporating features that are relevant to the 21st century

Project 1 - My Prusa i3 Mk2.5s 3D Printer

I have been printing with my Prusa 3D printer (pictured above) for over 3 years now. The Prusa line of printers originates from the Czech Republic and arrived at my house as kit. This led to a lot of future tinker and upgrades with my printer. It has been very helpful for academic and personal projects. One of my favorite prints I made with it was a spikey red planter that I keep my Japanese peace lily in. In the near future, I hope to add a livestream of my printer in action using Octoprint and a Raspberry Pi.
